Fettucini Alfredo

a beloved Italian pasta dish renowned for its rich and creamy sauce. It typically consists of fettuccine noodles tossed in a sauce made from butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese. The sauce is luxuriously smooth and coats the pasta generously, creating a dish that is both comforting and indulgent. Fettuccine Alfredo is often garnished with freshly ground black pepper and chopped parsley, offering a balance of flavors that highlight the simplicity and richness of the dish.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces (225g) fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)

Equipement

  • Large pot for boiling pasta
  • Large skillet or saucepan
  • Colander or strainer
  • Tongs or pasta fork for tossing pasta
  • Grater for Parmesan cheese

Preparing :

Preparing the Pasta

  • Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil over high heat.
  • Add a generous amount of salt to the boiling water.
  • Add the fettuccine pasta to the boiling water and cook according to package instructions until al dente (usually about 8-10 minutes).
  • Reserve about 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.

Preparing the Sauce

  • While the pasta is cooking, prepare the Alfredo sauce.
  • In a large skillet or saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  • Once the butter is melted, pour in the heavy cream. Stir constantly and bring to a simmer.
  • Reduce the heat to low and gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring continuously until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth and creamy. This process usually takes 3-5 minutes.
  • Season the sauce with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste. Adjust the consistency with reserved pasta water if needed (a few tablespoons at a time).

Assembling

  • Add the cooked and drained fettuccine directly to the skillet with the Alfredo sauce.
  • Gently toss the pasta with the sauce using tongs or a pasta fork until well coated and heated through. This allows the pasta to absorb some of the sauce and flavors.

Tips

  • Use freshly grated Parmesan cheese for the best flavor and texture.
  • Be careful not to overcook the sauce once you add the cheese to avoid separation.
  • Adjust the thickness of the sauce with pasta water until you reach your desired consistency.
Enjoy your homemade Fettuccine Alfredo as a comforting and satisfying pasta dish! !
