Lasagna

A traditional Italian dish made of layers of wide flat pasta sheets alternated with various fillings such as tomato sauce, meat (like ground beef or sausage), vegetables (like spinach or zucchini), and cheese (typically ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan). The layers are often topped with additional cheese and baked until golden and bubbly. It's known for its rich, hearty flavor and comforting texture.

Ingredients

For "The Meat Sauce"

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 450g (1 pound) ground beef
  • 450g (1 pound) italian sausage (ground pork)
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 oz each) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15oz) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

For "The Ricotta Mixture"

  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Assembling

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 450g (1 pound) mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Equipement

  • Large skillet or saucepan
  • Large pot
  • Mixing bowls
  • 9x13 inch backing dish
  • aluminum foil
  • Ladle
  • Whisk or Fork

Preparing :

Preparing the Meat Sauce

  • Heat Oil :

    • 1.1 : In a large skillet or Saucepan, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat.

  • Cook Onion and Garlic:

    • 2.1 : Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet.

    2.2 : Cook until the onion is tender and translucent, about 5 minutes

  • Brown The Meat

    • 3.1 : Add the ground beef and ground pork to the skillet.

    3.2 : Cook Until the meat is brownedand cooked through, breaking it up with spoon as it cooks. Drain excess fat.

  • Add Tomatoes And Seasoning :

    • 4.1 : Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce and water.

    4.2 : Add sugar, basil, italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and 1/4 cup of chopped parseley.

    4.3 : Simmer, uncovered, for about 1 and 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.

Preparing The Ricotta Mixture

  • Mix Ricotta

    • 5.1 : In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 cup of chepped paresley.

    5.2 : Mix until well combined. Set aside

Cooking the Noodles

  • Boil Noodles

    • 6.1 : Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil.

    6.2 : cook lasagna noodles in boiling water fro 8 to 10 minutes.

    6.3 : Drain noodles and rinse with cold water. Set aside.

Assembling The Lasagna

  • Preheat Oven

    • 7.1 : preheat your oven to 190 C (375 F) .

  • Layering

    • 8.1 : Spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce on the bottom of 9x13 inch baking dish.

    8.2 : Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce.

    8.3 : Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.

    8.4 : Top with a third of the shredded mozzarella cheese.

    8.5 : Spoon 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup parmesan cheese.

    8.6 : Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese.

Baking

  • Cover And Bake

    • 9.1 : Cover with aluminium foil.

    9.2 : Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes

  • Uncover And Finish Baking

    • 10.1 : Remove Foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Allow lasagna to cool for 15 minutes before serving

Tips

  • Letting the lasagna rest before serving helps it to set and makes it easier to cut into neat portions.
  • You can prepare the lasagna a day ahead and refrigerate it, then bake it when you are ready to serve.
  • For added flavor, use mix of ground beef and italian sausage
